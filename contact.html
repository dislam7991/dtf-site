Common Questions

FAQ

What are your minimum order quantities?

We offer some of the most competitive MOQs in the industry to support emerging brands. MOQs vary by product type and format — contact us for specific details based on your product category.

How long does the process take from concept to delivery?

For private label products, lead times typically run 3–6 weeks from order confirmation. Custom formulation projects vary depending on complexity but our team works hard to move as quickly as possible without sacrificing quality.

Do you handle label design and packaging?

We can support label development and connect you with trusted design partners. You're also welcome to bring your own artwork — our team will provide detailed label specifications and review for compliance.

Are your products third-party tested?

Yes. All products are manufactured in our cGMP-certified facility with full batch records and certificates of analysis (COA). We can coordinate third-party lab testing upon request.

Can I request samples before committing to a full order?

Absolutely. We encourage sample development as part of our process. Custom formulation projects include prototype samples for your review and approval before production begins.

Do you work with international brands?

While our facility is U.S.-based, we work with domestic and international brands. Our team can help navigate import/export requirements and documentation for international shipping.
