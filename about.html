Our Story

Built on a
Simple Principle

Dynamic Technical Formulations (DTF) was founded on a simple principle: supplement brands deserve a manufacturing partner as passionate about their product as they are.

We saw too many brands struggle with manufacturers who treated them as just another order number — slow communication, rigid processes, and zero investment in product quality. We built DTF to be the opposite.

Our team of formulators, flavor scientists, and manufacturing specialists bring decades of combined expertise to every project. Whether you're launching your first SKU or scaling an established line, we show up with the same level of dedication and rigor.

We thrive at the intersection of clinical precision and market innovation — where great science meets great business outcomes for our brand partners.
